The word 'Menorah' is taken from the Greek word of the lampstand; one of the instruments that was placed in the tabernacle of the Lord Almighty where His people worshipped and He came to dwell amongst them in the book of Exodus.

El Menorah is simply translated as the lampstand of God and as the name suggests, this app seeks to be a tool for throwing light; revelation, illumination and inspiration that the word of God carries to His children.
